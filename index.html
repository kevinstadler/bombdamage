This map shows data extracted from a historical map documenting war damage to buildings in Vienna around 1946 as a semi-transparent overlay over various other modern and historical maps. It allows one to get both a macro-level impression of the geography of the destruction across Vienna, as well as investigate micro-level questions such as how specific building sites destroyed during the war are used today.
